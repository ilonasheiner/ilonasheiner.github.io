HELLO!
Welcome! My name is Ilona. I am photographer from Lviv. Bold colours and ideas are what defines me and my work. I like to create new things on the regular, break boundaries and experiment with my art. As an artist, I solidified my foundations in photography in Lviv National Academy of Arts, specialising in Fashion and people. With my work & as an influencer I intend to inspire and bring about an actual change in society no matter how small or big. Photography for me is not looking, it’s feeling.
